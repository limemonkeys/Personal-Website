Proxy

Noun

An unofficial card used in place of an official card. Proxies require the enforcement of one of the most import rules to casual play: Rule 0. Proxies may vary in quality from sharpie alters to near replica of the official print. Regardless of quality of the proxy, the purpose remains the same: to make trading card games accessible to all.
